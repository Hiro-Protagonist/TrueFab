SHOPPING INFORMATION

Can I order by telephone?

You can always place your order by telephone. Call 1.800.750.8783 or 206.624.3195 for telephone order. Our office operates at 8:00am - 5:00pm Pacific Standard Time through Monday to Friday. If you're an existing wholesale customer, you can also order by emailing your customer service representative or by ordering online.

May I order catalog items online?

You may order any catalog items online. Enter the item number in the search box on our site or by using the quickorder feature. If you are wholesale and log in to wholesale, you may also view the catalog online.

How can I find out the status of an order placed online?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with your invoice and tracking number inside. To find when the products will be delivered, simply copy and paste the tracking number into www.google.com for an estimated arrival date.

If you have not received an email with your tracking and billing information, please email us at consumer@truefabrications.com to receive this information.

Where else can I find your products?

True Fabrications sells its products in over 5,000 retail wine stores, wineries, and grocery stores. If you cannot find our products in your local store, you can always order online or suggest we contact your local store by emailing us at consumer@truefabrications.com

How long will it take to receive my order?

It typically takes 24-48 hours for your order to ship. We ship using Fedex Ground services from Seattle, WA. It typically takes 3-7 business days for your products to arrive to your location. For expedited services, please make sure you call us at 800-750-8783.

Does True Fabrications donate products to non-profits or charities?

We do often donate our products to local (Seattle, WA) charity events. If you are a local charity, please contact us at consumer@truefabrications.com. You will need to come pick up products from our warehouse.

We also offer charities and non-profits heavily discounted products if you are not local. Please contact us with the item and quantity you are interested in along with a short description of your event and charity.

Can I make a change or cancel my order?

To change or cancel your order, please contact us at consumer@truefabrications.com within 24 hours of your order to make any changes or cancelations

Can I purchase or redeem gift certificates online?

Unfortunately, at this time, we do not offer gift certificates for our products. However, you can find our products in over 5,000 retailers where you can possibly purchase gift certificates from those individual stores.

Can I gift wrap items in my order?

We do not currently offer gift wrapping or gift notes with our orders.

What forms of payment are accepted for online purchases?

We accept all major credit cards for online purchases. If you are a wholesaler, you can also use a check or electronic check if you have been approved for terms.

Can I customize items?

You can customize products with your name or logo on many of our items. We do have minimums and setup charges depending on the item. For more information please email custom@truefabrications.com with the item number, units desired, and your logo in a high resolution format.

When will I receive a catalog?

True Fabrications creates catalogs only for wholesale and retail customers. If you have ordered from us in the past you will automatically receive these catalogs as they are produced. If your address changes or you are a new buyer, please contact us to update your information.

SHIPPING INFORMATION

What shipping options are available?

We currently ship all of our orders using Fedex Ground Services which takes 6-9 business days to arrive depending on your distance from Seattle, WA. For expedited orders, you will need to contact us at 800.750.8783. If you would like to use your own shipping account number or information, please also contact us.

Do you have any handling or additional surcharges?

We do not have any other charges other than basic shipping charges.

What is your policy on products that arrive broken or damaged?

True Fabrications undrestands that many of our products are fragile and may be damaged in transit. In such cases, please contact us at 1.800.750.8783 or 206.624.3195 8:00am - 5:00pm Pacific Standard Time, Monday - Friday and a customer service representative will assist you in exchanging and replacing those items.

Does True Fabrications ship to Hawaii, Alaska, and Puerto Rico?

We do ship items to Hawaii, Alaska, and Puerto Rico

Do you ship to Canada?

We currently only ship to the US unless you are a retail/wholesale customer.

Can orders be shipped to a P.O. box?

We cannot currently ship to PO boxes as we use Fedex Ground Services. You must provide a physical address for delivery.

Our Multi Brand Story


True Fabrications was founded in 2003 with a single humble wine bag. In the intervening years, our product line has grown to span every sector of the beverage industry and reach further than we could have imagined, from kitchen to décor to housewares. To address consumers’ equally wide-ranging needs and tastes, we’ve crafted a family of brands that appeal to unique consumer segments through distinctive product selection and proprietary packaging design. Together they provide more flexibility than ever in building a product selection for your unique needs and audiences.

Backed as always by our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee, our complete brand family makes us the leading designer and supplier of beverage lifestyle accessories worldwide, and continues to represent everything you know and love about True Fabrications.
